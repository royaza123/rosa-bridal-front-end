Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

When you visit our website or make a purchase, we collect:

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your custom dress orders
  • Provide customer service and support

Information Sharing

We don't sell your personal information. We only share with:

  • Service providers: Payment processors, shipping companies
  • Legal requirements: When required by law

Your Rights

You can request to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.
