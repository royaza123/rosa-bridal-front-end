Shipping & Returns Policy

Last updated: January 2025

📦 Shipping Information

Processing Time

  • Ready-to-Ship Items: 1-3 business days
  • Custom Dresses: 8-12 weeks from order confirmation
  • Rush Orders: 4-6 weeks (additional fee applies)
  • Alterations: 2-3 weeks after dress arrival

Shipping Options

🚚 Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): FREE on orders over $500

📦 Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $25

✈️ Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $50

🌍 International Shipping: Calculated at checkout

Packaging

Every dress is carefully packaged in our signature garment bags with tissue paper and protective materials to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

Custom Wedding Dresses

⚠️ Important: Custom-made wedding dresses are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged due to their personalized nature. Exceptions are made only for manufacturing defects.

Ready-to-Wear Items

✅ Return Window: 14 days from delivery date

📋 Conditions:

  • Items must be unworn and unaltered
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact
  • No signs of wear, damage, or odors
  • Return authorization number required

Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Email aetophis@aetophis.com with your order number
  2. Get Authorization: We'll provide a return authorization number and instructions
  3. Package Item: Securely package the item in original condition
  4. Ship Back: Send to our returns address (customer pays return shipping)
  5. Receive Refund: Processed within 5-10 business days after we receive the item

🌍 International Orders

  • Customs & Duties: Customer is responsible for all customs fees, duties, and taxes
  • Delivery Time: 7-21 business days depending on location
  • Tracking: International tracking provided for all orders
  • Returns: International return shipping costs are customer's responsibility

📞 Order Issues

Damaged or Defective Items

If your dress arrives damaged or has manufacturing defects:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange for repair, replacement, or full refund

Lost or Stolen Packages

  • We're not responsible for packages marked as delivered by the carrier
  • Check with neighbors, building management, or carrier
  • File a claim with the shipping carrier if package is confirmed lost
  • We'll assist with insurance claims when applicable

💰 Refund Information

  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days after item is received
  • Refund Method: Original payment method used
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping fees are non-refundable
  • Partial Refunds: May apply if item shows signs of use
